Contributing to CNSP
####################

Please join us! There are many ways to help the CNSP initiative, such as sharing your datasets or toolboxes
on our website, improving the CNSP resources on our GitHub, joining the initiative as a volunteer or as an
organised of the CNSP workshops, by presenting your work at CNSP, or simply let us know if you are finding
the CNSP resources useful. We would love to get your feedback! For example, please feel free to contact us on Twitter/X (@CnspWorkshop) or at
cnspworkshop [ a t ] gmail [ d o t ] com.
